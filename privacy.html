Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 22, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the TallyUp app ("Application") for mobile devices, created by Jordan S. Johnson ("Service Provider") as a free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

Information Collection and Use

The Application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data you create within the Application, including shopping trips, items, catalog names, budgets, and preferences, is stored locally on your device only. The Service Provider has no access to this data and cannot retrieve it.

The Application does not collect your location, IP address, device identifiers, usage analytics, or any other personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Services

The Application does not use any third-party services that collect or process your data. No data is transmitted to any external service.

Opt-Out

You can stop all use of the Application by uninstalling it. Since no data is collected or transmitted externally, uninstalling the Application removes all locally stored data from your device.

Data Retention

All user data is stored locally on your device. The Service Provider does not retain any of your data on external servers. If you uninstall the Application, all locally stored data will be deleted.

Children's Privacy

The Application is not directed at children under the age of 13. Since the Application does not collect any personal data, no data from children can be inadvertently gathered. If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns, please contact the Service Provider at jjohnson183dev@gmail.com.

Security

All data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally. Your information is protected by your device's own security measures, including passcode, biometrics, and device encryption.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Application after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the Service Provider at jjohnson183dev@gmail.com.
